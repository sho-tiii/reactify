Hafnium

Transition Metal

178.49 u

5,461 °C

Discovery
1923

Discovery

Discovered by Dirk Coster and George de Hevesy in 1923.

Common Uses

  • Used in nuclear reactors as a neutron absorber.
  • Used in the aerospace industry for heat-resistant materials.
  • Used in the production of high-temperature alloys.

Interesting Facts

  • Hafnium is almost always found in zirconium minerals.
  • It is used in various applications due to its high melting point and resistance to corrosion.
